"The Rats Have Risen"

“When restaurants closed, rats had to scavenge outside more. They found gutters and street-corner baskets clogged with trash because of cuts in the Sanitation Department Budget Illegal dumping increased. With most people stuck at home, so did residential waste.” (Shanahan, The New York Times, 2021) This, among other factors, such as a wetter than normal summer season and a general warming climate, has helped rats thrive under the pandemic circumstances. Even prior to the pandemic shifting the way New York City operates, it is evidenced by NYC open data that rat sightings increase during the warmer months, as a result of both people spending more time outside as well as rats’ preference for a warmer atmosphere.
